Tax Tips

  • Keep records: Maintain all income and expense records for at least 6 years.
  • File on time: Submit returns by the due date to avoid penalties.
  • Claim all deductions: Ensure you're maximizing legitimate deductions.
  • Update TIN: Keep your Tax Identification Number current.

Important Deadlines

  • PAYE: 10th of following month
  • VAT: 21st of following month
  • Withholding Tax: 15th of following month
  • Annual Returns: 31st March each year

FAQ

What is Consolidated Relief Allowance?
CRA is 20% of gross income or ₦200,000, whichever is higher. It provides automatic relief for all taxpayers.
When do I need to register for VAT?
You must register for VAT if your annual turnover exceeds ₦25 million. Registration should be done within 6 months of reaching this threshold.
What is the penalty for late filing?
Penalties vary by tax type but can include fines, interest on unpaid tax, and potential criminal liability for willful evasion.
